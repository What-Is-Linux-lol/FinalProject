About Me

Hello! My name is Nathan Jenkinson. I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity at Northern Michigan University, graduating in 2027.

I’m passionate about hands-on learning in information security — from building reproducible workflows and virtualization labs to exploring red team strategies and incident response. My focus is on SOC, MDR, and vendor-side InfoSec roles where I can apply both technical rigor and strategic thinking.

Outside of academics, I enjoy fishing in Michigan’s inland lakes, experimenting with Linux and Windows setups.

My goal is to combine technical expertise with practical problem-solving to help organizations strengthen their security posture while continuing to grow my own skills in the field.
